About Sorilamis

Our journey began in the winter of 2022 inspired by the crackling fire of a wooden wick candle. We've embarked on an extraordinary adventure, exploring the world of candle-making through a myriad of materials, waxes, and woods to bring to you the first candle with a heart shaped flame. The heart of our brand beats with passion, love, and creativity.

Our Candles

We believe that every candle we create is a testament to the artistry of the craft. We craft each candle, infusing it with the finest ingredients and with the promise that every Sorilamis candle you bring into your home will illuminate with love every moment that matters. Together, we'll create moments that shine bright and memories that last forever.
